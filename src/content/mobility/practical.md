## Checklist on arrival

- [ ] Residence registration (deadline and office)
- [ ] University enrolment / student card
- [ ] Health insurance validation (EHIC or host policy)
- [ ] Local bank account or fee-free card
- [ ] Transit pass (student discount usually needs the student card first — order matters)
- [ ] SIM / eSIM
