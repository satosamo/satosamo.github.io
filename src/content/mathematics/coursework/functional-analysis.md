## Scope

Notes following the functional analysis lecture course. Core results covered:

- **Hahn–Banach**: every bounded functional on a subspace extends to the whole space without increasing its norm.
- **Open mapping**: a surjective bounded operator between Banach spaces is open.
- **Uniform boundedness**: pointwise-bounded families of operators are norm-bounded.

## A first taste

A normed space $X$ is **Banach** if it is complete. The dual $X^* = B(X, \mathbb{R})$ is always Banach, even when $X$ is not, since

$$\|f\| = \sup_{\|x\| \le 1} |f(x)|$$

inherits completeness from $\mathbb{R}$.
