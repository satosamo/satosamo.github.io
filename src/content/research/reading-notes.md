## How this section works

One file per paper or per theme. Each note records the claim of the paper, the technique, and at least one open question it raises.

## Template

> **Paper.** Author, *Title*, year.
> **Claim.** One sentence.
> **Technique.** One paragraph.
> **Question.** What does this leave open?
